What's the ultimate guide to a healthy life

The common-sense, short version for basic, good health is as follows: Eat diversely: not the same thing every day. Also eat fruits and vegetables as much as possible. Don't eat more than feels good, though. Exercise. The more the better, but three hours total every week is a good minimum to go for. An exercise is good if you break a sweat doing it. Sleep enough and consistently. 8 hours minimum. Getting your pattern to hold even during weekends will make Mondays significantly more pleasant. Brush teeth twice every day and floss once. Have healthy, strong relationships with people. Having good friends is the foundation of a good mental health.
