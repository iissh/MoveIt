About Us

Our social networking site MoveIt! is a recently innovated social media platform designed to assist the people of Canada to live healthier lives. There are no judgements here. We seek only to encourage, empower, and entertain people with their health and quality of life with confidence. We foster a culture of constant teaching, learning and development so that you can grow both mentality and physically as an individual.

Our Goals

Our goal is to use this platform to promote healthy living in Canada, using popular aspects of several social media, giving the users options to post, share and comment on MoveIt! MoveIt will promote healthy diets, workouts that can be done at home and help promote awareness regarding mental health within these very stressful times.
